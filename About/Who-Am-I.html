Hacking

I am passionate about hacking since november 2020 when I found the website hackthebox.eu and got myself an account in.

That information may not seem interesting but the website needed you to "hack your way in" to get an account and as I had no knowledge of hacking before I was very proud of me for achieving that.

That month was important to me because I learned a ton of new things and I got a new passion in that.A year later, I'm now more advanced in hacking and even took part in hacking competitions (called CTF for Capture The Flag) : one organized by the french air force called mars@hack in march 2021 , one called  HeroCTF  in april 2021, and the last one I took part in was the  San Diego CTF in may 2021. 

I learned a lot of things with those challenges and I met people all over the world and we collaborated to win. I was super hyped about it. I made a team to take part in those competitions : its name is  404 Found (you can find our position to the last two CTFs on that page).


Now my objective in hacking is to always lean something new and get a job in CyberDefense or to work in the french army as a CyberSecurity officer.
