Places I lived

I was born near Paris but when I was 9 years old my parents went to Tahiti due to work. So, I basically spent half my life there, because I came back in France this year, being 18 years old.

Where is Tahiti

For those who are a bit lost in geography, Tahiti is a small island located in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. It’s a part of French Polynesia, a country which is basically the size of half the European Union.

How is living in Tahiti like ?

Of course the way of living there isn't the same as living in metropolitan France even though we speak the same language.

Social interactions

To start off, the first thing I noticed when I arrived in France was how everyone was really minding how they were looking and such. No one would take risks to look awkward. It was really bugging me because in Tahiti we just don't mind that, we can do a lot of things that would be looked down upon in France.
As for an example, here is when the principal of my high school changed, the new one took part in a traditional dance (Haka) even though he didn't know any of it.

December 7th, 2018, at the Lycée Diadème, Tahiti :
The Marquesans students performed a Haka to welcome the new principal

Moorea

Tahiti is famous for its beaches, but it’s actually a big lie. Tahiti doesn’t have the prettiest white sand beaches, in my opinion. It only has 2 white sand beaches by the way, all the other beaches are black ones because of the volcanic type of rocks that made the sand.
The prettiest beaches are on Tahiti sister’s island, Moorea, only a few tens of kilometers away from Tahiti, you have to take the Ferry to go there.

I didn’t go there that many times because it’s still an expense but this island is what we look to when we want the “postal card” type of landscape.
That’s the island where you have high-quality hotels and transparent blue lagoon. 
I really enjoyed the time I spent there, I swam with a dolphin, fished some seashells, ate pineapples. The fact that it was very cheap (because I was staying at a friend and we ate what we found in the yard) made every vacation trip very fun.
